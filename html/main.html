Hello! We're Clarkson University's chapter of
the Society of Women Engineers (SWE).

THE SOCIETY OF WOMEN ENGINEERS (SWE)

was founded in 1950 in an effort to empower women
scientists and engineers. This organization values
professional excellence, integrity, and mutual support.
Since this organization's founding, there are
now around 50,000 members worldwide.

CLARKSON'S CHAPTER OF SWE

was founded in 1973, making it the first chapter
of SWE in New York state. Our chapter aims to
strengthen inclusivity while providing academic,
professional, and social support for women
in engineering at Clarkson.
